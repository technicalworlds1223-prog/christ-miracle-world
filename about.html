About Christ Miracle World

Christ Miracle World (CMW) is a Christ-centered, Spirit-filled digital ministry committed to spreading the everlasting Gospel of Jesus Christ in Telugu and English. Founded with a divine calling to reach nations through media and prayer, our mission is to lead souls into salvation, strengthen believers in faith, and proclaim the miraculous power of God to the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a global spiritual family rooted in prayer, worship, and the uncompromised Word of God. We believe in raising disciples who live by faith, walk in righteousness, and shine as light in this generation.

Through powerful sermons, testimony videos, daily Bible verses, and missionary stories, we strive to bring transformation into families, youth, and communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preach the Gospel to every home and every heart through digital outreach. We provide Telugu Christian Messages, Gospel teachings, Bible clarifications, missionary life stories, and testimonies of God's miracles.

We are dedicated to prayer support, spiritual counseling, and encouraging believers to grow deeper in Christ through consistent fellowship and worship.

What We Believe

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, Savior of the world, and the only way to eternal life. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the moving of the Holy Spirit.

We stand on God’s promises and proclaim that miracles still happen today through faith in Christ Jesus.

“I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me.”
— John 14:6
